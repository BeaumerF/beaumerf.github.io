About me

I'm a passionate game developer from Rennes (FRANCE), who loves turning ideas into fun and engaging projects.

Creating games is more than my job — it's my craft. I enjoy working on every step of the process : from clear game design to 3D modeling, rigging, and animation, through to UI design, level creation, and above all, programming. My goal is always to build experiences that are as visually appealing as they are exciting to play.
